About the Event

Welcome to the Tech Quest event at SPARKFEST 2024! This event is a thrilling challenge that tests your general knowledge and quick thinking through a series of quizzes, including a Picture Quiz. It's open to all JITIANS and promises to be a fun and intellectually stimulating experience.

The event will be held on the 11th and 12th of November 2024, from 8:45 AM to 3:00 PM at the JIT Campus. Get ready to put your knowledge to the test and compete with the brightest minds!

Rules

Participants must adhere to the following rules:

  • Each team can have up to 4 members.
  • All answers must be submitted within the allotted time.
  • No use of mobile phones or any external help during the quiz.
  • Decisions made by the quiz masters are final.
