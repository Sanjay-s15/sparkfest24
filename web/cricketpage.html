About the Event

Get ready for the ultimate cricket auction experience at SPARKFEST 2024's IPL Auction! This thrilling event will give you a taste of the high-stakes action and strategic bidding that fuels the excitement of the Indian Premier League. Form your dream team and compete to become the best franchise owner.

The IPL Auction will take place on the 11th and 12th of November 2024, from 8:45 AM to 3:00 PM at the JIT Campus. It's an event that combines strategy, knowledge of cricket, and a dash of drama. Don't miss out!

Rules

Participants must adhere to the following rules:

  • Teams can consist of up to 4 members.
  • Each team will have a set budget for bidding.
  • Bidding will be conducted in rounds, and all bids must be submitted within the allotted time.
  • No external assistance or use of mobile phones during the auction.
  • Decisions made by the auctioneer are final.
