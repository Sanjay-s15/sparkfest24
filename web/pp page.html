About the Event

Join us for the Paper Presentation event at SPARKFEST 2024, where innovative minds from across Jeppiaar Institute of Technology will come together to showcase their research and ideas. This event offers a platform for students to present their papers on a wide range of topics, providing an opportunity to gain valuable feedback and recognition from peers and industry experts.

The event will take place on the 11th and 12th of November 2024 at the JIT Campus. It is open to all JITIANS. Register now to be a part of this exciting academic exchange and inspire others with your work!

Rules

Participants must adhere to the following rules:

  • Papers must be original and submitted by the author(s).
  • Each presentation should not exceed 15 minutes.
  • Participants should bring a printed copy of their paper and a USB with their presentation.
  • Any form of plagiarism will result in disqualification.
