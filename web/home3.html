About the Event

Welcome to SPARKFEST 2024, where innovation ignites the future! This flagship event, hosted by the IEEE Student Branch at Jeppiaar Institute of Technology, is a dynamic fusion of intellect and excitement, set to take place on the 11th and 12th of November. Our event brings together the brightest minds from various disciplines to collaborate, compete, and celebrate the spirit of technology and creativity.

This year, SPARKFEST boasts a diverse lineup of events designed to challenge and inspire. From high-stakes coding contests and thought-provoking paper presentations to the exhilarating IPL Player Auction and an array of engaging workshops, there's something for every tech enthusiast. Attendees will have the opportunity to network with industry experts, showcase their skills, and gain insights into the latest technological advancements.

Beyond the competitions, SPARKFEST 2024 is a celebration of camaraderie and community. It's a platform for students to push the boundaries of innovation, share their passions, and build lasting connections. Whether you're a participant, a spectator, or a volunteer, you'll leave with a wealth of knowledge, unforgettable memories, and a renewed passion for technology.

Join us at SPARKFEST 2024 and be a part of a transformative experience that not only fosters personal and professional growth but also ignites the spark of creativity and innovation within each of us. Let's create, compete, and celebrate together at Jeppiaar Institute of Technology!
