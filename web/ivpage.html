About the Industrial Visit

Join us for an insightful Industrial Visit to the Tamil Nadu Smart and Advanced Manufacturing Centre (TANSAM), powered by Siemens. This visit, organized by the IEEE Student Branch at Jeppiaar Institute of Technology, is an exciting opportunity to witness cutting-edge technology and advanced manufacturing processes.

The visit will take place at Tidel Park, Chennai. Participants will get a firsthand look at the latest innovations in smart manufacturing and gain valuable industry insights.

Visit Schedule

The schedule for the visit is as follows:

  • 8:00 AM - Departure from Jeppiaar Institute of Technology
  • 9:30 AM - Arrival at TANSAM, Tidel Park
  • 10:00 AM - Welcome and Introduction
  • 10:30 AM - Guided Tour of the Manufacturing Centre
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch Break
  • 1:00 PM - Interactive Session with Industry Experts
  • 2:30 PM - Q&A Session
  • 3:00 PM - Return
