About the Event

Unleash your inner adventurer at SPARKFEST 2024's Tech Treasure Hunt! Dive into a world of clues and challenges designed to test your wit and teamwork. Explore the campus, solve puzzles, and unearth hidden treasures.

The Tech Treasure Hunt will take place on the 11th and 12th of November 2024, from 8:45 AM to 3:00 PM at the JIT Campus. Gather your team and get ready for a day filled with excitement and adventure!

Rules

Participants must adhere to the following rules:

  • Teams should consist of 3-5 members.
  • Follow all clues in the correct order.
  • Respect the campus and do not damage any property.
  • No use of mobile phones or external help during the hunt.
  • All team members must stay together during the hunt.
